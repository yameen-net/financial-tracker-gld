About ClearCash

ClearCash is a streamlined financial tracker designed specifically for young adults who are beginning their financial journey. We provide simple yet powerful tools to help you monitor your spending, understand your budgeting habits, and visualize your overall net worth growth effortlessly.

Our Mission

At ClearCash, we aim to simplify personal finance management. Financial literacy shouldn't be complicated; therefore, we strive to deliver an intuitive experience to empower young adults to make informed financial decisions and achieve financial independence.

Key Features

  • Budget Tracking: Categorize and track your monthly income and expenses to maintain a healthy financial balance.
  • Net Worth Visualization: Clearly visualize the changes in your financial status over time through easy-to-read graphs.
  • Currency Conversion (Coming soon!): Quickly convert your financial figures between multiple currencies (currently supports GBP, USD, EUR).

Who We Are

We are a team of students passionate about providing accessible financial solutions for our peers. Understanding the financial hurdles young adults face today, we've tailored ClearCash to be easy to use, highly practical, and fully transparent.
